The Spider Pulsars Catalogue

This is a catalogue of spider pulsars. These are neutron stars in binaries with low mass companions. It is complementary to the catalgoue of black holes in X-ray transients (Blackcat), the catalogue of black hole masses detected in gravitational wave events (LIGO/VIRGO mass plot), and the catalogue of dormant black hole systems (DormantBHCat).

It was created as part of the Spiders ERC porject

The background image is the Spiders group logo for Rene Breton's research group at the University of Manchester.
