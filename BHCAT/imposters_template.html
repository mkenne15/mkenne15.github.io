Imposter Black Hole Binaries catalogue
Dormant Blackholes

This is a catalogue of imposter binaries that have at one stage or another masqueraded as a dormant black hole binary.
It was created during The Renaissance of Stellar Black-Hole Detections in The Local Group meeting at the Lorentz Centre, June 26-30 2023.
